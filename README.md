# Sustainability Garden Guide

A practical guide to growing your own food with full nutritional coverage for one man and one woman.

## Project Goals

### Goal 1 — Nutritional Requirements
Simplified daily/yearly nutritional intake requirements for:
- An adult man
- An adult woman

### Goal 2 — Annual Growing Guide
A complete growing guide listing all plants needed to meet the nutritional requirements from Goal 1.
Includes standard garden vegetables and annual crops.

### Goal 3 — Perennial Growing Guide
A no-replanting alternative using only perennial plants:
- Trees
- Bushes/shrubs
- Perennial herbs and ground covers
- Focus on legacy and heirloom varieties for high nutrient density
