Goal 3 — Perennial Garden Plan

Plant once. Harvest for decades. Every plant here regrows on its own — no annual replanting. Priority given to heirloom, legacy, and open-pollinated varieties with the highest known nutrient density. Build this system alongside the annual garden while trees establish.
